Income AI Assist — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Income AI Assist is an informational, educational estimate tool. It is designed to run entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no login, and no server database. Anything you type, and any paystub or document you scan, is processed locally on your device. Your information is not transmitted to us or to any third party, and we have no ability to access it.

Document scanning

When you scan a paystub or W-2, the text is read on-device using an in-app reader. The one network exception is that, the first time you scan, the app downloads the text-recognition engine from a public software host — your document itself is never sent; only the engine is fetched.

Data storage & deletion

Your entries live only in the current app session and your device’s local storage. You can clear them anytime with the in-app “Clear” action; the app also auto-clears after a period of inactivity. Removing the app deletes its local data.

Camera & photos

Camera and photo-library access are used only so you can scan or select a document image to read on-device. Images are not uploaded.

Not advice

Results are estimates for informational and educational purposes only. They are not financial, tax, or lending advice, not a loan approval or pre-qualification, and not a commitment to lend. Always consult a licensed mortgage professional.
